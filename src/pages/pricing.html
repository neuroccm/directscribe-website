What's included, precisely

Recording and note creation carry no DirectScribe-imposed limit, and templates are unlimited. Transcription itself is billed separately by your chosen vendor's API — see the cost breakdown. Support runs for the life of your subscription and includes custom feature requests, as long as they don't change the app's overall nature or how it's designed to work.

Prices in Canadian dollars. A licence key is delivered by email; there is no account to create and no server that can lock you out mid-clinic.

The honest all-in number

Software fee plus your own API spend

Incumbent scribes bundle the AI cost into one monthly price. DirectScribe separates them, so you see exactly what you pay and can pick a cheaper vendor. On a sensible stack, most solo physicians spend about CAD $10–15/month in API usage on top of the software fee.

Why separate the costs? Because bundling hides the markup. When the AI bill is yours, you can switch to a cheaper transcription vendor, run note-shaping locally, or scale down in a quiet month — and DirectScribe's price never changes.
