Cost breakdown

What it really costs, all-in

DirectScribe's software fee is fixed. The rest is your own vendor spend, which you can see and control. These static tables use figures verified in July 2026 for a solo private practice of roughly 440 notes per month.

These are estimates, not quotes. Vendor prices change; audio length, note length, and patient volume all move the number. Treat the ~440-notes column as the anchor and the lighter/heavier columns as scaled estimates. Re-check the current rates with each vendor before you rely on them.
